# JOBVEJ V1 Privacy Notice

Date: 2026-04-18

## Status

This is the V1 privacy notice text for the controlled pilot release.

Before public go-live, replace the support placeholders below with real contact details.

## Short version

JOBVEJ lets you explore municipalities and save personal follow information if you choose to create an account.

If you use an account, the app stores only the information needed to:

- let you sign in
- remember your followed municipalities
- remember your saved searches
- protect the service against misuse

## What we store

If you create an account, JOBVEJ stores:

- your email address
- your optional display name
- a password hash
- your language/locale preference
- your followed municipalities
- your saved searches
- operational and security logs related to authentication and abuse protection

JOBVEJ does not use your account to build a broad personal profile about you.

## Why we store it

We store account and usage data to:

- authenticate you
- show your own follows and saved searches
- protect the service against abuse
- maintain and troubleshoot the service

## Who can access it

- you can access your own follows and saved searches after login
- administrators can access admin tools needed to operate the service
- technical operators may access operational data when needed for support, troubleshooting, or security review

## How long we keep it

For V1:

- account data is kept until you ask for deletion or support initiates an approved deletion
- follows and saved searches are deleted when your account is deleted
- security and audit events are normally kept for 180 days
